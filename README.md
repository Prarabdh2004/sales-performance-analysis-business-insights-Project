# sales-performance-analysis-business-insights-Project
Power BI and Data Analysis project focused on sales performance optimization, profitability analysis, and business recommendations.

## Problem: 
Company is facing an Inconsistent Sales Performance and decline in Profitability in certain regions and Product Category.

## Objective: 
- Is to find the Root cause of the problem.
- Recommend Strategies to improve Revenue & Profitability.

## Insights:
- Technology category contributes highest revenue and profit. This symbolises category should be prioritized for expansion and marketing investment.
- Furniture category shows low profitability despite sales, likely due to high discounts. This indicates a need for pricing or supply chain optimization.
- Higher discounts in the Central region are not generating proportional profit. This indicates ineffective discount strategies and potential revenue leakage.
- West region is the top-performing region in both sales and profit, indicating strong market presence and operational efficiency.
- Profitability declines sharply when discount exceed 20% and discount above 30% consistently results in negative profit. This indicates excessive discounting leads to revenue leakage.



## Root Cause Analysis:
- Excessive discounting without corresponding growth in sales volume is leading to margin erosion.
- Poor pricing strategy in Furniture category.
- Regional demand differences not aligned with strategy. 


## Business Analyst Recommendation:
- Implement discount cap at 20% to prevent margin erosion & ensure profitability
- Optimizing pricing of Furniture Products Category by reducing excessive discount.
- Replicate successful strategies from West region in Central region.


## Stakeholder Analysis:
| Stakeholder | Objective | Key Concern |
|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| Sales Manager | Increase sales & profitability | Declining profit margins |
| Marketing Team | Optimize campaign effectiveness | Ineffective discounts |
| Operations Team | Reduce operational costs | High costs impacting profit |
| Customers | Value for money | High prices or poor service |


## User Story:
- As a Sales Manager, I want to analyse region-wise Sales & profit so that I can identify underperforming regions and improve sales performance.
- Acceptance Criteria:
- System should display region-wise sales and profit 
- Should highlight low-performing regions 
- Should allow filtering by category

## AS-IS vs TO-BE State:
- AS-IS:
High Discount → Low Profit → No Control
- TO-BE:
Optimized Discount → Controlled Pricing → Improved Profit


## Business Requirement Document (BRD):
- The system should provide region-wise sales and profit analysis to identify performance trends.
- The system should identify loss-making products and categories to support pricing decisions.
- The system should analyse the impact of discount levels on profitability to optimize discount strategies.
- The system should consist data filtering and visualization by region, category, for better decision-making.
